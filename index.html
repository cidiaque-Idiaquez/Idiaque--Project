Welcome to the Festival Fairy!

About Us

Festival Fairy is your ultimate guide to the electronic dance music (EDM) festival experience. We celebrate the music, culture, and community that brings festival-goers together from around the world.

Our Mission

To share the joy and energy of music festivals while promoting the P.L.U.R. philosophy (Peace, Love, Unity, Respect). We provide resources to help both new and experienced ravers make the most of their festival adventures.

What is a Festival?

The basic definition of a festival is a celebration or event that brings people together to enjoy music, food, and activities, but are also known as Raves.

What is a Rave?

In the past raves were underground parties that were often held in secret locations, such as warehouses or abandoned buildings. They were known for their high-energy music, colorful lights, and a sense of community among attendees. Today, raves have become more mainstream and are often held in larger venues, such as concert halls or outdoor festivals.
