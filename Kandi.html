What is Kandi?

Kandi is another name for braclets that ravers make to trade with one another. They come in all different sizes and designs from singles to epic cuffs.

How do you trade Kandi?

To trade kandi with another person you both do a sort of special handshake while saying the words Peace, Love, Unity, Respect. Each word is associated with a certain hand motion.
For example
for PEACE you place the peace sign up with the other person.
for LOVE you and the other person make a heart with your hands.
for UNITY you place your palms flat against the other persons palm.
for RESPECT you intertwine you fingers as to grab there hand.
at this time during the kandi trade youy both pass the kandi you want to gift the other person.
